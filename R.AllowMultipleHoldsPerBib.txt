R.AllowMultipleHoldsPerBib

----------

Name: AllowMultipleHoldsPerBib

----------

Options: 

----------

Description: This allows multiple items per record to be placed on hold by a single patron. To enable,enter a list of space separated itemtype codes in the field (i.e. MAG JMAG YMAG). Useful for magazines,encyclopedias and other bibs where the attached items are not identical.

----------

Type: 

----------

"LOCALHOLD"
